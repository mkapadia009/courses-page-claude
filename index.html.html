Why Study Shri Atmasiddhi Shastra?

Shri Atmasiddhi Shastra stands as one of the most profound spiritual texts ever written, offering a clear roadmap to self-realization through six fundamental truths. This course brings together the timeless relevance of this 19th-century Jain scripture with modern pedagogical approaches.

Guided by Pujya Gurudevshri Rakeshji

Pujya Gurudevshri Rakeshji is an enlightened Master, spiritual visionary, modern-day mystic, and humanitarian leader. Born on 26 September 1966 in Mumbai, he showed signs of divinity from a very young age.

An ardent devotee of Shrimad Rajchandraji, he founded Shrimad Rajchandra Mission Dharampur in 1994, which has now grown to encompass 206 centres across six continents. Blending theory with experience and head with heart, Pujya Gurudevshri provides powerful and practical tools for the inward journey.

  • Ph.D. from University of Mumbai (research on Shri Atmasiddhi Shastra)
  • Master's in Philosophy with Gold Medal from University of Mumbai
  • Completed 5-year bachelor's degree in 3 months
  • Gandhi Seva Medal recipient (2017)
  • Honorary Doctor of Divinity from Claremont School of Theology (2023)
  • Founded Shrimad Rajchandra Love and Care (UN consultative status)
  • Transforming millions globally through wisdom and service
